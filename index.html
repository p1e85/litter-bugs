Key Features in the Alpha

Track Your Impact

Use your phone's GPS to draw your cleanup path on an interactive map. Every step is a victory for the planet.

Pin Your Finds

Found some litter? Take a photo to geotag its location. Your data helps create a global heatmap of problem areas.

Earn Tips & Badges

The community can tip you for your hard work via your support link! Plus, unlock achievement badges for your cleanup milestones.

Join the Community

Publish your routes to a shared map to see the collective impact we're making together and compete on the community leaderboard.

Be the First to Know

Join our mailing list for updates on our official launch, new features, and the project roadmap. Help us build the future of cleanups!
